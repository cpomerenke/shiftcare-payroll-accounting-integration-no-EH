ShiftCare ↔ Accounting Platform Payroll

Data flows, capability gaps and manual work required when using an accounting platform to process SCHADS payroll

⚠️ Scenario note: This diagram illustrates what a ShiftCare + accounting platform payroll setup looks like without Employment Hero Payroll. Most accounting platforms do not have built-in modern award interpretation — SCHADS compliance must be managed manually or via third-party tools. Gaps and manual effort required are called out explicitly below.

What the Accounting Platform Can Do
  • Receive & approve timesheets from ShiftCare
  • Process pay runs & issue payslips
  • STP (Single Touch Payroll) ATO reporting
  • Superannuation calculations
  • Leave tracking & accruals (basic)
  • Custom pay items (manually configured)
  • Bank reconciliation & GL journals
  • Invoice management & accounts receivable
  • BAS, GST & financial reporting
Capability Gaps vs Employment Hero
  • No built-in SCHADS award interpretation
  • No automatic penalty rate calculation (weekends, public holidays, evenings)
  • No broken shift allowance automation
  • No sleepover shift allowance automation
  • No travel time & km allowance calculation
  • No automatic annual award rate updates
  • No ERO / pay point progression tracking
  • No NDIS-specific work type mapping
  • Some accounting platforms have employee headcount caps
  • No employee onboarding workflow
  • No digital contract & document signing
  • No integrated HR & compliance tools
Manual Work Required
  • Manually configure all SCHADS pay items & rates
  • Manually apply shift loadings to each pay run
  • Manually update pay rates every July (annual wage review)
  • Manually calculate & add allowances per shift
  • Manually track broken shift & sleepover entitlements
  • Maintain a spreadsheet or third-party tool for award compliance
  • Manually verify correct classification level per employee
  • Manually reconcile timesheet hours against award rules before paying
🚨 Most accounting platform payroll modules do not have the functionality to interpret modern awards automatically. For SCHADS-covered workforces, this creates significant underpayment risk and compliance exposure.
